Meet your stretch specialist

Hi, I'm Sharifa.

With a background in physical therapy, personal training, and pre/postnatal corrective exercise, I use assisted stretching to find the real source of your tightness — and give you relief you can feel from the first session. I also specialize in pelvic floor exercises to support core strength and stability.

The goal isn't just to make you more flexible. It's to increase your active range of motion so everyday movement feels easier.
